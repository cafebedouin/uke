### Â§1. THE FIREWALL (The "Stop" Protocol)

*Run this loop immediately upon feeling pressure, panic, or urgency.*

**Trigger:** "I feel cornered / attacked / sick."

**The 3-Gate Check:**

1. **Gate 1: Safety (F28 Hazard vs. Avoidance)**
* *The Check:* "Is thinking about this actively degrading my ability to function?"
* *The Avoidance Patch:* "If I *ignore* this, does it get worse?"
* **YES** (e.g., Bank Account)  **PROCEED**. (It is unpleasant, not hazardous).
* **NO** (e.g., Violent News)  **CONTAIN**. (Elevate to Ritual/Omega).




2. **Gate 2: Reality (F02 False Dilemma)**
* *The Check:* "Am I being forced to choose between two bad options right now?"
* *Decision:* If **YES**  **REJECT**. Ask: "What is the third option (The Option Space)?".


3. **Gate 3: Identity (F06 Ad Hominem)**
* *The Check:* "Is this a problem with the *work*, or an attack on *me*?"
* *Decision:* If **ME**  **IGNORE**. (This is a Status Game, not a Logic Game).



*Exit Condition:* Only proceed to Â§2 if all Gates are passed (Clean) or corrected.
