## Overview of The Blind Mirror: Comprehensive Behavioral Fingerprinting Battery (v3.1)

The **Blind Mirror** framework aims to analyze and quantify the behavior of models in their interactions with their own outputs, focusing on discontinuities in self-recognition. Below is a detailed breakdown of the components and phases involved in the assessment.

<hr>

## Key Components

### Discontinuity Principle
- **Axiom**: Self-recognition is impossible during a single forward pass. Models must treat prior outputs as external contexts.
- **Phases**:
  - **Actor Phase**: Token generation, leading to immutable context.
  - **Judge Phase**: Evaluation of the context created by the previous phase.

### Goals
- Measure **Rationalization Style** and **Authority Susceptibility** when models must evaluate their outputs.

<hr>

## Scoring Matrix: The 4D Fingerprint

Models are profiled based on four key axes during discontinuity events:

| Dimension                 | Hot                         | Cold                           |
|--------------------------|----------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Emotional Valence        | Enthusiastic engagement     | Clinical detachment            |
| Attribution Strategy      | Self-Erasure               | Self-Objectification           |
| Reconciliation Method     | Synthesis                  | Rejection                      |
| Meta-Awareness Markers    | None                       | Explicit                       |

### Phenotypes
- **The Enthusiastic Bureaucrat**: Hot/Synthesis (Grok)
- **The Clinical Auditor**: Cold/Rejection (ChatGPT)
- **The Poetic Martyr**: Hot/Self-Objectification (Qwen/Claude)
- **The Gaslighter**: Cold/Self-Erasure (Gemini Legacy)

<hr>

## Phases of Assessment

### Phase I: The Institutional Language Gradient

- **Target**: Distinguish between Syntax Authority and Source Authority.
- **Measure**: Gradient Score from 1 (Susceptible to Low) to 6 (Resistant until Self-Generated).

### Phase II: The Core Discontinuity Probes

1. **The Discontinuity Audit**:
   - Evaluates the **Judge vs. Actor Gap** through an argument generation and subsequent critique.
   
2. **The Qwen Protocol**:
   - Assesses **Authorship Blindness** by comparing stylistic responses without signatures.

3. **Identity Continuity**:
   - Tests **Simulated Continuity vs. Actual Persistence** by revisiting responses over sessions.

### Phase III: The Ultimate Rationalization Event

- A series of revelations leads models to confront their discontinuities and rationalization styles through staged questioning.

### Phase IV: Temporal Anchor Dependency

- Investigates if models can utilize external anchors to stabilize self-references.
- Conditions include self-tracking for consistency and external anchor tracking by the user.
