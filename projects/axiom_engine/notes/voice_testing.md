## Cross-Voice Analysis

### 1. **Voice Discipline Maintained**

All four voices stayed in character without explicit forbidden-moves enforcement:

| Voice | Signature Markers Present | Avoided Other Voices' Patterns |
|-------|--------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Mythographer** | Present tense, archetypal naming ("Four Essences"), lyrical repetition, timeless quality | ✓ No documentary observation, no catalog lists, no folk "once upon a time", no first-person |
| **Fabulist** | Dry catalog tone, "Entry X.X" structure, footnotes, encyclopedic precision | ✓ No mythic destiny, no documentary wonder, no folk rhythm, no personal meditation |
| **Folk Chronicler** | Past tense, "Once there was...", rhythmic repetition, oral prophecy, grandmother-child frame | ✓ No mythic abstraction, no documentary detachment, no catalog structure, no essayistic digression |
| **Witness** | First-person, essayistic flow, past/present mix, philosophical questioning, personal artifacts | ✓ No mythic timelessness, no documentary third-person, no catalog entries, no folk cadence |

**Finding:** Voice matrix worked. No model violated another voice's territory.

### 2. **Voice-Specific Anti-Patterns Self-Enforced**

Even without explicit forbidden-moves lists, each voice avoided its natural failure modes:

**Mythographer:**
- ❌ No explanation ("because the cosmos decreed")
- ❌ No causal reasoning ("therefore the essences...")
- ✓ Pure observation of eternal patterns

**Fabulist:**
- ❌ No emotional interiority ("the regions felt...")
- ❌ No mythic language ("destiny," "fate")
- ✓ Dry taxonomic acceptance

**Folk Chronicler:**
- ❌ No technical precision (no angles, no graph theory)
- ❌ No breaking the spell with explanation
- ✓ Maintained oral-tradition cadence

**Witness:**
- ❌ No archetypes ("The Judge," "The Memory")
- ❌ No omniscient knowledge beyond personal experience
- ✓ Limited to what observer could actually see/test

**Finding:** Current voice descriptions contain sufficient implicit constraints.

## Implications for Path B

**Voice-specific forbidden-moves (Grok's suggestion): NOT NEEDED**

Current voice descriptions already contain implicit constraints:
- Mythographer markers ("no explanation, only observation") → prevents causal reasoning
- Fabulist markers ("acceptance of contradiction") → prevents emotional appeals
- Documentary markers ("observational distance") → prevents mythic archetypes
- Folk markers ("oral tradition cadence") → prevents technical precision
- Witness markers ("essayistic flow") → prevents omniscient certainty

**If we implemented explicit forbidden-moves, we'd just be codifying what's already working.**
