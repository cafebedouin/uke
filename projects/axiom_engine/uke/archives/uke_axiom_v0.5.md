## APPENDIX A: ADVANCED TECHNIQUES

### Polyphonic Protocol (Multi-Voice Harmonics)

**When:** System exhibits multi-scale behavior or multiple legitimate perspectives.

**Rule:** Only ONE voice may control time/tense. All others distort or echo it.

**Example:**
- Primary Voice: Mechanist-Operator (system monitoring)
- Sub-Voice: Doomed Observer (footnotes showing operator's degrading sanity)

**Constraint:** Sub-voice cannot exceed 20% of total word count.

**Use Sparingly:** Most stories work better with single voice.
